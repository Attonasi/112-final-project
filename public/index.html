Use your time Wisely

Unless you are a professional athlete you don't have 8 hours a day to workout. You have 1 or 2 hours 4 or 5 days a week. To be honest I can show you how 1 hour a day 4 days a week is enough if you are consistent and you execute a well developed strategy.

Core Exercize Philosophy

The core philosophy is high intensity training and heavy full body motion weight training. These are the workouts that consistently result in the most effective outcomes in scientific studies. Curls and Jogging are the cheeseburgers of workouts. Do workouts that make your stronger and more conditioned.
